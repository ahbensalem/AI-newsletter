## Phase Details

### Phase 1: Full Build
**Goal**: The redesigned newsletter is live with all three pages, the editorial design system, and the sticky sidebar TOC — readers can browse editions, navigate via TOC, and view the archive
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase)
**Requirements**: FOUND-01, FOUND-02, FOUND-03, FOUND-04, LAYOUT-01, LAYOUT-02, LAYOUT-03, LAYOUT-04, LAYOUT-05, LAYOUT-06, LAYOUT-07, LAYOUT-08, PAGE-01, PAGE-02, PAGE-03, PAGE-04, PAGE-05, PAGE-06, RESP-01, CONT-01, CONT-02, CONT-03
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A reader can open the home page and see a hero section, featured article highlight, and article cards with category badges and reading times
  2. A reader can navigate to a monthly edition page and use the sticky sidebar TOC to jump directly to any article section, with the active section highlighted as they scroll
  3. A reader can open the archive page and see all past editions in a card grid with edition metadata (month, year, issue number, article count)
  4. The navbar is frosted glass, sticky at the top, and all navigation links work; the footer is dark with newsletter branding
  5. The layout adapts correctly across mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports with no broken layouts

**UI hint**: yes

### Phase 2: Responsive Polish
**Goal**: Mobile users get a proper hamburger navigation and the sidebar TOC gracefully collapses or hides on small screens; performance is production-ready
**Depends on**: Phase 1
**Requirements**: RESP-02, RESP-03
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. On a phone-sized viewport the navbar shows a hamburger icon that opens and closes a mobile navigation menu
  2. On a phone-sized viewport the edition page hides the sticky sidebar TOC and provides an alternative way to access article sections (collapsed panel or equivalent)
